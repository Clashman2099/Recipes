Taco

Description

The Taco is an easy-to-make recipe for lunch. Just collect the following ingredients from the list:

Ingredients

  • 2 steak pieces
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tspn salt
  • 1 tspn pepper
  • 1 tspn oil
  • 4 tortillas

Process

  1. Cut the steaks into small pieces
  2. Add the oil to a stovetop
  3. Cook the steaks to the stovetop
  4. Cut the onion to smal pieces
  5. Put the steaks and onions to the tortillas
  6. Put salt and pepper over the tacos
  7. Ready to eat
