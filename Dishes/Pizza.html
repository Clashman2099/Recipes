Pizza

Description

The Pizza is an easy-to-make recipe for party and reunions. Just collect the following ingredients from the list:

Ingredients

  • 300 gr flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tspn salt
  • 2 tspn sugar
  • 300 ml. water
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • Mozarrella cheese
  • 1 bag of pepperonni

Process

  1. Add and mix flour, eggs, salt, sugar, and water
  2. Massage the pizza's mass until being circular
  3. Add tomato sauce, mozarrella cheese, and pepperonni respectively
  4. Put the pizza to the oven in 300 C for 45 minutes.
  5. Take out the pizza and ready to eat.
